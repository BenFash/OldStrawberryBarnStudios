About us

We moved to Cheddar in October 2020 and over the next 9 months, fully renovated the 4 studios as well as undertaking major works on the 1708 cottage next door. We’re delighted with the results and our guest reviews have been so positive along with numerous customer choice awards from Cottages.com. We take great pride in how the studios are presented and each one has a 2 hour clean between guest stays

The Cheddar community is really welcoming and pretty much everything is no more than 15 minutes walk from the studios. We’ve built up a good knowledge of the best places to visit and have tested most of them ourselves – pubs, a great range of restaurants, local beaches, the Cheddar Ales taproom, England’s smallest city (Wells), walks in the Mendip Hills, to Crooks Peak or the Gorge and Glastonbury Tor. What a range of things to do right on our doorstep! Our studios offer a lovely blend of rural tranquility and convenience with proximity to all Cheddar offers

We have a friendly, tennis ball loving labradoodle and 2 of the 4 studios are dog friendly. One well behaved dog is welcome in each

If you love the outdoors, great pubs and are looking for a relaxing getaway, come and visit us at The Old Strawberry Barn Studios in Cheddar. We look forward to welcoming you!
