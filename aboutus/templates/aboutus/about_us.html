About us

We, Nick and Kerry, are delighted to invite you to our charming collection of four fully refurbished studio apartments, nestled alongside our own cottage dating back to 1708. Located on the edge of the historic Somerset town of Cheddar, our studios offer the perfect blend of rural tranquility and convenience with proximity to local attractions. Just a 10-minute walk from the world-famous Cheddar Gorge, you'll find great pubs and places to visit within walking distance.

We have two friendly dogs - a black lab and her daughter, a labradoodle so please keep this in mind if your dog is nervous or reactive.

If you love the outdoors, great pubs and are looking for a relaxing getaway, come and visit us at The Old Strawberry Barn Studios in Cheddar. We look forward to welcoming you!
